How does this work?

What We Do

In an era of speed and digitalization, we strive to keep alive a very simple and warm method of communication
– handwritten letters.

We listen to your thoughts, feel your emotions and transform them into words on a piece of paper and deliver it to your dear ones.

Be it any occasion: anniversary, congratulatory message, festive wishes or just getting back in touch after a long time; we will write a letter for it.

All you have to do is fill in your details.. could be send voice mails, images, or anything that will help us understand your intent better to put them on paper.

If you already have a draft in mind, that’ll be marvellous!

How does this work?

The Only Thing You've Got To Do

Below the description, you will come across a link; a link that will take you far away.
You know where you hope the link will take you; but, you can be sure that you will be one step closer to giving your details.

The link is divided in multiple sections meant to collect different kind of details that will
help us in knowing you, understanding your purpose and knowing the person/group for whom the letter is to be written.

How we do, what we do.

  1. 1. The first one of the links is for knowing about the event/occasion for the letter.

  2. 2. In the next one, you will come across a provision to upload your audio, text files, images that will help us write the content of the letter. You can record the audio and/or write the text instantaneously but the pictures should be on your device already to upload.

  3. 3. Next will be your and receiver’s basic details followed by the Paytm payment link.
